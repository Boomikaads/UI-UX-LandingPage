Dynamic Island

For when things just pop up, Dynamic Island bubbles up alerts and Live Activities — so you dont miss them while you’re doing something else. You can track your next ride, see who is calling, check your flight status and so much more.

When something bubbles up, just touch and hold to act on it. Dynamic Island expands so you can find out when your dinner will arrive, rewind your podcast or see if your AirPods have connected and their battery level.
